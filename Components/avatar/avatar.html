Avatars

Make Responsive Avatars with Muffin UI. Various templates like a circle avatar, square and letter avatar. Avatars are used widely across the Website for profiles, blogs and many more.

Image Avatars

Image Avatars are available in 4 sizes large, medium, small and x-small. To use the avatars, just include classname img-fit to your image for responsiveness and avatar. Also as per the requirement you can also add avatar-lg, avatar-md, avatar-sm, avatar-xs as class names. You can copy the html code from below code snippet. You can add image of your choice.

Letter Avatars

Letter Avatars are also available in 4 sizes. These avatars display the Initials of the person. To use the letter avatars, just include classname avatar-initials to div and avatar. Also as per the requirement you can also add various sizes.
